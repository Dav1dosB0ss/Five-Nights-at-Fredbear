ENG: WARNING! Made by Gemini 3.1 Pro, GPT 5.5, Claude Haiku 4.5! RUS: ВНИМАНИЕ! Сделано с помощью Gemini 3.1 Pro, GPT 5.5, Claude Haiku 4.5!

ENG: This is a fan game based on the Five Nights at Freddy's franchise, set in 1983 at Fredbear's Family Diner, called Five Nights at Fredbear's. It's roughly the same as Five Nights at Freddy's 2, with two side vents and a front passage. The game features two animatronics, Springbonnie and Fredbear. Springbonnie is active and crawls through the left and right vents. To protect yourself from Springbonnie, you need to locate it in one of the vents by turning on the light with the white button next to each vent and close the vent with the red button. After a while, Springbonnie will leave. Fredbear will only appear in the front passage, and when his energy runs out, to protect yourself from him, you need to locate him in the front passage using the flashlight (Ctrl on PCs/laptops and the button on phones/tablets). Once he's spotted, immediately put on his Fredbear mask, and he'll leave after a while. Good luck in the game!
